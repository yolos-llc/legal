Privacy Policy — YOLOs SNS Insights

Last updated: July 17, 2026

YOLOs SNS Insights ("the App") is an internal analytics tool operated by YOLOs LLC ("we", "us"), a social media management agency based in Fukushima, Japan. The App retrieves social media performance statistics for accounts whose owners have explicitly authorized access.

1. Information We Collect

With the account owner's authorization via TikTok Login Kit, the App accesses the following data through the TikTok Display API: basic profile information (display name, avatar), account statistics (follower count), and the account's own video metadata and statistics (views, likes, comments, shares). We do not collect passwords, private messages, or data about other users.

2. How We Use Information

The data is used solely to produce performance reports and improvement recommendations for the account owner (our client) as part of our social media management service. We do not sell, rent, or share the data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Retrieved statistics are stored on our internal systems and are retained only while the client relationship is active. Data is deleted upon the client's request or contract termination.

4. Revoking Access

Account owners can revoke the App's access at any time from their TikTok app settings (Settings > Security > Apps and websites) or by contacting us.
